Navy-Style Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 300g minced meat
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onion, and fry until golden.
  3. Add minced meat to the pan and cook until browned.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Mix the cooked pasta with the meat mixture.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
