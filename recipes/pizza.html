Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 pepperoni or any preferred topping
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese and your choice of toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.).
  6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and drizzle with a little olive oil.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, add fresh basil leaves on top, and serve hot.
