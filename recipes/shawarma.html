Shawarma

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast (or lamb, beef, or pork)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita or flatbread
  • Fresh vegetables (tomato, lettuce, cucumber)
  • Tahini sauce or garlic sauce

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with the spices (cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, salt, and pepper) for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the chicken until golden brown on both sides and cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken and slice it into thin strips.
  4. Warm the pita or flatbread.
  5. Assemble the shawarma by placing chicken, vegetables, and sauce on the bread.
  6. Roll it up and enjoy!
